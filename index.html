Emoji Stickers - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 21, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our Emoji Stickers application. By using the application, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information Collection and Use

Emoji Stickers collects minimal data and values user privacy:

  • Favorite Stickers: The app stores your favorite sticker preferences locally on your device only.
  • Device Storage Access: If you create custom stickers, we may need to store these images on your device.
  • Internet Access: The app uses internet access to load emoji images.

Personal Data and Cookies

Emoji Stickers application:

  • Does not collect personal data from users
  • Does not use third-party analytics tools
  • Does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies
  • Does not send user data to external servers
  • Does not share user data with third parties

Local Storage

The application stores your preferences (favorite stickers, custom stickers) using SharedPreferences only on your device. This data is deleted when you uninstall the application and is not shared with anyone else.

Children's Privacy

Emoji Stickers is suitable for all age groups and respects children's privacy. Since we do not collect any personal data, concerns regarding the protection of children's personal information are minimized.

App Permissions

Our application only requests permissions that are absolutely necessary for proper functioning:

  • Internet access: To load emoji images
  • Storage (optional): To store custom stickers

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
